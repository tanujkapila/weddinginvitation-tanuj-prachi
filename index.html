Getting there

Travel information

Everything you need to know to join us in Sonipat.

By air

Flight

Indira Gandhi International Airport (IGI) is the nearest airport, approximately 60 km from Sonipat.

  • Drive time: ~90 minutes
  • Taxis and car rentals available
  • We recommend pre-booking transport

By road

Driving

Sonipat is well-connected via National Highway 1, about 45 km north of Delhi.

  • Drive time: ~1 hour from central Delhi
  • Ample parking available at venue
  • GPS coordinates provided upon request

By train

Railways

Sonipat Railway Station connects to major Indian cities and is within walking distance of the venue.

  • Multiple daily trains from Delhi
  • Local taxis available from station
  • Journey time from Delhi: ~40 minutes

Common questions

FAQ

Everything you might want to know.

What should I wear?

For the engagement, semi-formal ethnic attire is suggested. For the wedding, formal ethnic wear is recommended. Traditional Indian clothing is perfect for both occasions.

Can I bring my children?

Yes! Children are very welcome at both events. Please let us know in your RSVP if you'll be bringing children so we can make appropriate arrangements.

Is accommodation provided?

We are arranging special rates at nearby hotels for our out-of-town guests. Details will be shared via email shortly after your RSVP.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Please mention any dietary preferences or restrictions in the RSVP form. We'll ensure special arrangements are made for all our guests.

Can I bring a plus-one?

We'd love to have your family and close friends! Please let us know the number of guests you'll be bringing in your RSVP so we can plan accordingly.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive 15–30 minutes before the start time. Parking is available on-site, and our team will be there to welcome you warmly.
